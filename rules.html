Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. Follow Minecraft's official rules – no hacked clients, cheats, or unfair advantages.
  2. Be respectful to all players and staff. No hate speech, bullying, or toxic behavior.
  3. No griefing or stealing unless allowed in the specific gamemode (e.g., Lifesteal).
  4. Do not spam, advertise, or post inappropriate content in chat or signs.
  5. Use only allowed mods (e.g., OptiFine). Any unfair mod will result in a ban.
  6. Do not exploit bugs or glitches – report them to staff immediately.
  7. Impersonating staff or other players is strictly forbidden.
  8. Follow staff instructions at all times – their word is final in all disputes.

Discord server

  1. Be respectful to everyone – no hate speech, racism, sexism, or toxic behavior.
  2. No spamming, flooding, or excessive tagging in any channel.
  3. Keep conversations in the correct channels – read channel descriptions first.
  4. No advertising, self-promotion, or sharing invite links without permission.
  5. Do not impersonate staff or other members.
  6. Use appropriate usernames and profile pictures – no offensive content.
  7. Do not ping staff unnecessarily – use support channels when needed.
  8. Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!

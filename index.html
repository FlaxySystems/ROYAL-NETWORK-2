Who we are?

Royal-Network is a rising Minecraft server built for players who love action-packed gameplay and a vibrant community. Our main gamemode right now is Bedwars, where strategy, teamwork, and PvP skills are key to victory. But that’s just the beginning — we're actively working on adding Lifesteal SMP, where players gain power by stealing hearts, and Skyblock, a classic island survival experience with a competitive twist. Whether you're here to fight, build, or make new friends, Royal-Network is your place to play, grow, and rule. Join us and become part of a server that’s made by players, for players.

Bedwars

Battle in fast-paced Bedwars on Royal-Network! Defend your bed, destroy enemies, and dominate the arena. Play solo or with friends. Respawn until your bed is gone – then it’s do or die. Join now and become the last team standing!

Lifesteal

Survive and conquer in Lifesteal on Royal-Network! Every kill steals a heart, making you stronger – but die, and you lose one. Form alliances or go solo in this intense PvP survival world. Can you rise to power or lose it all?

Server FAQs.

Here you'll find quick answers to the most common questions about our server, gameplay, and features. Whether you're new or returning, this is the best place to get up to speed.

Q1: What is Royal-Network?
Royal-Network is a Minecraft server focused on fun, fair PvP and community-driven gameplay. We currently offer Bedwars, with Lifesteal SMP and Skyblock coming soon!
Q2: What version does the server support?
We support the latest Minecraft Java Edition versions, with compatibility for 1.8 – 1.20+ (best experience on 1.8.9 for PvP).
Q3: Is the server cracked or premium?
Both! Royal-Network is a cracked + premium server, which means players with or without an official Minecraft account can join and enjoy the same experience.
Q4: How do I earn rewards?
By voting daily, winning games, and participating in events, you can earn keys, coins, kits, and other in-game bonuses.
